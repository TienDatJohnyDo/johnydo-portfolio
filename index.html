I graduated with a degree in Software Engineering from the University of Calgary. My time there gave me a solid foundation in problem-solving, software development, and data analysis, but the projects and internships taught me just as much.

During my internship at CIBC, I delivered daily reports and conducted in-depth data analysis for senior leadership, helping drive strategic decisions through actionable insights. At H&R Block, I led the AI chatbot project for the DIY Tax software team where I was designing conversation flows, analyzing customer data, and collaborating across teams to improve the user experience.

Currently, as an Application Developer at IBM, I work on data migration, pipelines, and modeling, modernizing legacy enterprise systems so that teams can make data-informed decisions.

Outside of tech, I'm a big believer in staying active. I play in a volleyball league 4 times a week. I also film our matches and post them on YouTube!

Looking ahead, I'm excited to channel my technical and analytical skills into a role where I can help teams make smarter, data-informed decisions.
